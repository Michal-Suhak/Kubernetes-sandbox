To access your application:

{{- if .Values.fastapi.ingress.enabled }}

   echo "$(minikube ip) {{ .Values.fastapi.ingress.host }}" | sudo tee -a /etc/hosts

   http://{{ .Values.fastapi.ingress.host }}/
{{- else }}
1. Get the application URL:
   minikube service {{ .Release.Name }}-fastapi -n {{ .Values.namespace }}

To check pod status:
  kubectl get pods -n {{ .Values.namespace }} -l app=fastapi

To view logs:
  kubectl logs -f deployment/{{ .Release.Name }}-fastapi -n {{ .Values.namespace }}
